About Zoldoš Lab

Zoldos Lab is primarily interested in understanding genetic and epigenetic background of changes of protein glycosylation observed in complex diseases, such as chronic inflammatory diseases and diabetes. Also, we were among the first groups to develop dCas9-based molecular tools for epigenome editing. Using these tools, we dissect the network of genes regulating IgG glycosylation, genes involved in diabetes type HNF1A-MODY, and genes involved in chronic inflammatory diseases. We are also using the dCas9- based tools in studies of dynamics and persistence of epigenetic changes following manipulations in cells in culture, as well as to study the interplay between different epigenetic marks, such as DNA methylation and histone modifications, in gene regulation.
